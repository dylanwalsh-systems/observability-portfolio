How this demo works

A simple flow: alert → impact → evidence → stakeholder update.

1) Alert appears

Monitoring detects unusual behavior for a specific time window.

2) Confirm impact

We open Live View to confirm what users experienced and where.

3) Collect evidence

Charts and comparisons get captured for quick review.

4) Communicate

A ticket + email update is generated in plain English.
This is static and safe to share publicly.
